About Us


Thank you for visiting my online store. We are a family owned business who started over 50 years ago, when my grandfather fell in love with making acoustic music. He wanted to share his love with the community and started a small store in Miami, Florida. Over the last year we have expanded our presences across the globe with our online presence, while keeping my grandfathers store open for shopping, guitar lessons and live acoustic performances on the weekends. If you are in the area come check us out or tune in to watch our live performances that are streamed every Friday night!
